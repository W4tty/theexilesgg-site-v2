Our Modded Minecraft Server

ATM9 Exiles Edition: a custom All The Mods 9 pack with over 300 mods, custom questlines, and a community server to play it on.

Who We Are:

The Pack:

ATM9 Exiles Edition is our own build of All The Mods 9, running on Forge for Minecraft 1.20.1 with more than 300 mods. Tech, magic, and exploration are all in, along with new dimensions like the Twilight Forest, the Aether and the Undergarden. Whether you want to automate everything, chase the ATM Star, or just build something enormous with friends, the pack supports it.

New Player Guide

What's On The Server:

  • Custom Questlines: Server questlines including the Exiles Directive guide your progression through the pack.
  • Stargate Network: A working stargate travel network connects bases, outposts and dimensions.
  • Ranks & Perks: Progress through server ranks and unlock perks as you go.
  • Community Builds: A shared spawn island and plenty of neighbours worth visiting.
  • Full Documentation: The Exiles Codex covers the modpack, progression, travel and tools in detail.

Join The Server

The server is run for and by the community. If modded Minecraft is your thing, or you'd like it to be, you're welcome.

How to join: hop into our Discord, ask in the Minecraft channel, and follow the new player guide in the Codex to install the pack and connect.

Frequently Asked Questions

What modpack does the server run?

ATM9 Exiles Edition, our custom pack built on All The Mods 9. It runs on Forge for Minecraft 1.20.1 with over 300 mods covering tech, magic, exploration and new dimensions.

How do I install the pack and connect?

The new player guide in our Codex walks through installing the pack and connecting to the server step by step. If you get stuck, ask in Discord and someone will help.

What makes the server different from vanilla ATM9?

Custom questlines including the Exiles Directive, a stargate travel network connecting bases and dimensions, server ranks with perks, and a community spawn island.

How do I get access to the server?

Join our Discord and ask in the Minecraft channel. Server details are shared there.

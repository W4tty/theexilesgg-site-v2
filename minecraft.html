Our Modded Minecraft Server

ExilesMC Aero: our next custom modpack, in active development. From pedal power to airships to rockets, with a detour through a galaxy far, far away.

The Pack:

ExilesMC Aero is the modpack we're building right now: over 500 mods on NeoForge for Minecraft 1.21.1, shaped around a custom quest campaign that takes you from land to sky to space. The Create ecosystem powers the journey, with trains, factories, jetpacks and airships, until Creating Space puts you in a rocket bound for real solar-system planets. And then there's the Star Wars layer: whole planets to visit, flyable ships with hyperdrives, lightsabers and blasters. Underneath it all sits deep tech from Mekanism, Applied Energistics 2 and Powah, magic from Ars Nouveau and Apotheosis, and a world overhauled with Biomes O' Plenty, seasons and Distant Horizons vistas.

What's In The Pack:

  • Land, Sky & Space: A custom quest campaign carries you from pedal-powered contraptions to jetpacks, airships, and finally rockets to real solar-system planets.
  • Star Wars: Visit Star Wars planets, fly ships with working hyperdrives, and settle arguments with lightsabers and blasters.
  • Create Everything: The full Create ecosystem with trains, factories and flight, the engineering heart of the pack.
  • Deep Tech & Magic: Mekanism, Applied Energistics 2 and Powah for the engineers; Ars Nouveau and Apotheosis for everyone else.
  • A Better World: Biomes O' Plenty, Serene Seasons and Distant Horizons make the view from your airship worth the climb.
  • Built In-House: Pack, quests and even custom mods are developed by our own team. Member feedback shapes every update.

Coming Soon

Aero is in active development: quests, balancing and even a custom mod are being built by our team right now. The community server will switch over when it's ready.

Want in? Join our Discord and say hello in the Minecraft channel. You can play with the community today and be there on day one when Aero launches.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ExilesMC Aero?

Join The Exiled Titans: Our EVE Online Corp

Wormhole Life Awaits, Getting Rich Or Die Trying!

Greetings, Capsuleers! The Exiled Titans are calling all pilots to join our ranks: newbros, returning veterans, and seasoned pros alike. We're a growing corp based in wormhole (with 2 Vargurs 1 Hole) and high-sec near Jita for those who crave adventure.

What We Offer:

  • Mining Operations: Regular fleets for ore, ice, and gas in both high-sec and wormhole space. Boosts provided!
  • Wormhole Content: PvE, exploration, and opportunities to engage in WH shenanigans.
  • Industry Projects: Help build our ships and modules as we establish our industrial backbone.
  • Newbro-Friendly Environment: Learn the ropes with patient, experienced mentors.
  • Chill, Fun Atmosphere: No drama, just good vibes and epic EVE adventures.
  • Opportunities to Grow: Rise as a fleet commander, industrial manager, or recruiter. The sky's the limit.

What We’re Looking For:

  • • Players excited to help a corporation and alliance grow.
  • • Miners, explorers, industrialists, and combat pilots, there's room for everyone!
  • • Team players eager to contribute to our shared success.
  • • Both new players and veterans ready to learn and share their expertise.

Why Join The Exiled Titans?

Our corp is more than a group of players, it's a tight-knit community. We may be small, but our ambitions are huge. Based near Jita, we blend high-sec safety with the thrill of wormhole exploration.

Ready to Join?

Hop into our public channel, The Exiled Titan Pub, or message us directly in game for more info.

Together, we'll mine the riches of the stars, conquer wormhole mysteries, and build something great.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the corp based?

We live in wormhole space and keep a high-sec presence near Jita, so there's room for both quiet industry and genuine adventure.

Do you accept new players?

Yes, newbros are genuinely welcome. EVE rewards knowledge above all, and flying with experienced pilots is the fastest way to learn. We'll help you get set up.

What playstyles fit here?

Miners, explorers, industrialists and combat pilots all have a place. Rise to fleet commander, industrial manager or recruiter if that's your ambition.

How do I join?

Join our Discord, introduce yourself, and one of our EVE pilots will get you sorted.

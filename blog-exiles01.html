Why The Exiles is the Best Elite Dangerous Squadron for You

Looking for a squadron that feels like home? You’ve found it.

  • Author:
    Watty
  • Category:
    Elite Dangerous
  • Date:
    Feb 24, 2025

Elite Dangerous is a massive galaxy, and while flying solo has its charm, nothing beats the camaraderie of a dedicated squadron. That’s where The Exiles come in. We’re not just a group of Commanders, we're a family. Whether you’re a bounty hunter, trader, explorer, or just figuring things out, we’ve got a place for you.

What Makes The Exiles Different?

A True Community

Some squadrons feel like faceless organizations. The Exiles? We’re tight-knit. Our Discord is buzzing with activity, whether it’s planning missions, sharing in-game discoveries, or just having a laugh. No matter your experience level, you’ll always find support here.

Combat-Ready and Always in the Action

Love bounty hunting, PvP skirmishes, or system defense? We do too. The Exiles operate as a cooperative force, tackling dangerous factions, keeping control over systems, and making a name for ourselves. Need backup? You’ll never be alone in a fight.

Exploration and Trade Opportunities

Not every Exile is a warrior. Some are explorers and traders, mapping the unknown and running profitable trade routes. If you enjoy the thrill of discovery or the art of making credits, you’ll find mentorship and guidance here.

No Overly Strict Rules, Just Teamwork

We’re not a military-style squadron. We don’t have ranks that create unnecessary power structures. We just work together, support each other, and have fun. Whether you want to be active daily or pop in when you can, we welcome all kinds of players.

Why Join The Exiles?

  • Never fly alone again: Always have backup in combat or trade runs.
  • Grow your skills: Learn from experienced pilots and take your gameplay to the next level.
  • Shape the galaxy: Our squadron has influence, and we’re always expanding.
  • Be part of something bigger: More than just a squadron, we’re a family.

How to Join The Exiles

Becoming part of The Exiles is simple:

  1. Join our Discord: discord.gg/pW2AMFEHUc
  2. Introduce yourself: Tell us what you love about Elite Dangerous.
  3. Get in on the action: We'll help you get set up with squad missions, bounties, or exploration routes.

The Exiles’ Code: How We Operate in the Elite Dangerous Universe

What does it mean to be an Exile?

Elite Dangerous is full of squadrons, each with their own style. Some are strict military units, others are loose pirate gangs. The Exiles? We operate with a simple code: have fun, work together, and support each other.

The Exiles Code: What We Stand For
  • Teamwork Comes First: Whether it’s bounty hunting, exploration, or trade, we always help each other out. Lone wolves are welcome, but you’ll never have to fly alone if you don’t want to.
  • No Drama, Just Gaming: We're here to enjoy the game, not get caught up in politics or power struggles. Everyone’s voice matters, and we all contribute.
  • Respect for Fellow Commanders: We're a cooperative squadron, not a dictatorship. No unnecessary ranks, no egos, just a community of players helping each other thrive.
  • We Make Our Own Path: We're not bound to a superpower or major faction. We carve out our own legacy in the galaxy, shaping systems, taking on challenges, and forging our own destiny.

If this sounds like the kind of squadron you want to be part of, then welcome home.

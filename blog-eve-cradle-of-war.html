EVE Online: Cradle of War
New Eden Goes to War

CCP's newest expansion has launched, bringing military campaigns and a conflict-driven season to New Eden.

EVE Online: Cradle of War
The Expansion Has Landed

  • Author:
    Watty
  • Category:
    EVE Online
  • Date:
    Jul 2, 2026

CCP Games has released Cradle of War, the latest expansion for EVE Online, alongside patch 23.02. As the name suggests, the theme is conflict. The centrepiece is military campaigns, a rolling series of objective-driven operations that draw capsuleers into contested space, beginning with the Bulwark Mining campaign.

The launch season also brings new Empire SKINs, faction celebrations and the usual round of expansion events. Industrialists have a war economy to supply, and combat pilots have somewhere to point their fleets. Whichever side of that equation you sit on, there's a good reason to undock right now.

"EVE is at its best when the sandbox is contested, and Cradle of War is built to keep it that way."

The Exiles

New to New Eden?

EVE rewards knowledge more than any other game we play, and flying with an experienced group shortens the learning curve enormously. Our corp flies from wormhole space and high-sec near Jita, and our pilots are happy to help new players find their footing. Introduce yourself on Discord.

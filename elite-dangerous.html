Join Our Elite Dangerous Squadron

Are you ready to embark on an epic journey among the stars? The Exiles are calling out to brave pilots like you to explore the vast universe of Elite Dangerous.

Who Are We?

The Exiles is a close-knit squadron dedicated to every facet of the Elite Dangerous experience. Whether you're a seasoned commander or just beginning your journey, we welcome you with open arms.

We’re more than just a squadron; we’re a family of pilots who share a passion for PvE, some PvP, trade, exploration, BGS shenanigans, AX combat, and much more. Our Player Minor Faction allows us to shape the future of our own corner of the universe.

Why Join The Exiles?

  • Diverse Activities: Engage in trading, exploration, hunting Thargoids, or making your mark in Powerplay.
  • Experienced Pilots: Learn from veterans and get support with ship loadouts, trading routes, and tactics.
  • Close-Knit Community: More than a squadron, we're a group of commanders who value teamwork and camaraderie.
  • BGS Shenanigans: Manipulate the Background Simulation (BGS) and help our minor faction grow.
  • AX Combat: Team up with fellow commanders to take down Thargoids and protect humanity.
  • Galactic Rescue Ops: Assisting stranded commanders with fuel, repairs, and emergency evacuations to ensure no pilot is left behind.

Ready to Soar with The Exiles?

Elite Dangerous is vast, but you don’t have to explore it alone. Whether you're a trader, explorer, or combat pilot, The Exiles welcome you to a squadron where camaraderie and adventure go hand in hand.

Ready to forge your legacy among the stars? Join our ranks, team up with fellow commanders, and take part in thrilling missions that shape the galaxy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join The Exiles squadron in Elite Dangerous?

Join our Discord and say hello, or find The Exiles on Inara and request an invite in-game. There are no applications or interviews.

Do you accept new players?

Yes. Plenty of our commanders started with us as new players. Our Codex has getting-started guides, and experienced members will fly with you while you learn.

What does the squadron actually do?

Background Simulation (BGS) campaigns in support of our faction, wing missions, the new co-op Operations content, anti-Thargoid combat, and plenty of mining, trading and exploration.

Is there a minimum activity requirement?

No. Most of our members are adults with jobs and families. Play when you can, log off when you need to.
